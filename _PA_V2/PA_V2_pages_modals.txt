## ğŸ¯ KEY ARCHITECTURAL DECISIONS

### **Homepage Structure**
- Hero section: Video background (fades 0-4s) + Heading (fades 1s-3s) + Typewriter paragraph (2s-5s)
- Three animated cards: Community (left, 0s), Marketplace (bottom, 0.5s delay), Tools (right, 1s delay)
- Cards fade to 50% opacity after slide, text fades in
- Featured sections: Trending, Creators, New Releases, Top Services, Upcoming Webinars

### **Navigation System**
- **Header** (global): Logo, Search icon, Theme toggle, Notifications bell (auth), Avatar dropdown
- **Sidebar** (auth only): Slides out on hover (desktop), hamburger menu (mobile)
  - Items: Feed, Profile, Messages, Notifications, Groups, My Store, Wallet, Settings
- **Bottom Nav** (mobile auth): Feed, Explore, +Post, Notifications, Profile
- **Guest avatar**: Single "Sign Up/Log In" dropdown item
- **Logged avatar**: Duplicates sidebar items in dropdown

### **Search Strategy**
- Search icon opens modal (blur background, live results)
- Press Enter or "View All" â†’ `/search?q=[query]&tab=[tab]`
- One universal search results page with tabs
- Tabs use same templates as browse pages
- Default tab based on where user searched from

### **Profile System**
- Single Smart Link field (rolink.me only) - validated
- Profile tabs: About | Posts | Products | Services
- "View All My Links" button opens rolink.me in new tab
- Hybrid following: One-way = "Following/Followers", Mutual = "Connected" badge

### **Marketplace Structure**
- Unified marketplace with tabs: Products | Services | Webinars
- Same card templates used in search results, profiles, everywhere
- List/Grid toggle on all browse pages
- Advanced filters per category

### **Creation Flows**
- Posts = Modal (quick, stays on page)
- Products/Services = Dedicated page (multi-step, complex)
- Auto-save drafts every 30 seconds

### **Orders & Payments**
- Single `/orders` page with Purchases/Sales toggle
- Real-time status updates (Supabase Realtime)
- Stripe Checkout modal for payments
- Commission snapshot at purchase time

### **Registration Flow (Multi-Step)**
1. Account basics (email, password, username, location)
2. Roles & skills (checkboxes with expansion like image uploaded)
3. Genres & experience level
All in modal, progress bar at top

### **Theme System**
- Sun/Moon toggle in header
- User choice (not auto-detect)
- Saves to preferences
- Smooth 0.3s transition

### **Mobile Strategy**
- Bottom nav for primary actions
- Hamburger menu for secondary
- Sidebar becomes full-screen overlay
- Stack vertically, 2-column grids
- Touch targets 44px minimum

## ğŸ”² ALL MODALS DEFINED

### GLOBAL MODALS (Available Everywhere)
1. **Search Modal**
   - Trigger: Click search icon in header
   - Features: Live results, filters, Enter to see full results
   - Closes: Outside click, X button, ESC key

2. **Login/Register Modal (Multi-Step)**
   - Trigger: Guest clicks "Sign Up/Log In" or protected action
   - Login view: Email, password, "Forgot password?", Google OAuth
   - Register Step 1: Full name, username (check availability), email, password, location
   - Register Step 2: Roles (checkboxes with sub-options like image), Skills
   - Register Step 3: Genres (checkboxes), Experience level (radio)
   - Progress bar at top for registration
   - Closes: After successful login/registration

3. **Theme Toggle** (Not a modal, but header element)
   - Sun/Moon icon in header
   - Click to switch instantly

### PAGE-SPECIFIC MODALS

4. **Create Post Modal**
   - Trigger: Click "Create Post", "+What's on your mind?", or mobile + button
   - Features: Multi-line text, media upload, emoji, link preview, poll
   - Destination: Feed or Group dropdown
   - Auto-save draft
   - Closes: After posting or cancel

5. **Checkout Modal (Stripe)**
   - Trigger: Click "Buy Now" on product
   - Shows: Product info, price breakdown, commission
   - Stripe Payment Element embedded
   - Closes: After successful payment or cancel

6. **Share Modal**
   - Trigger: Click "Share" on post, product, profile, etc.
   - Options: Twitter, Facebook, WhatsApp, LinkedIn, Email
   - Copy link button
   - Closes: After action or outside click

7. **Report Modal**
   - Trigger: Click "Report" on any content
   - Reasons: Spam, Harassment, Inappropriate, Copyright, Scam, Other
   - Optional details textarea
   - Closes: After submission

8. **Confirmation Modal**
   - Trigger: Destructive actions (delete, leave, ban, etc.)
   - Shows: Warning message, "Are you sure?"
   - Actions: Cancel, Confirm (red button)
   - Closes: After action

9. **Message Modal (Quick)**
   - Trigger: Click "Message" on profile or product
   - Features: Text input, emoji, attach file
   - Pre-filled context (optional)
   - Option: "Go to Full Conversation"
   - Closes: After sending or cancel

10. **Image/Video Viewer Modal**
    - Trigger: Click image in post or product gallery
    - Features: Full-screen overlay, navigation arrows (if gallery)
    - Closes: Outside click, X button, ESC key

11. **Cart Modal** (Future)
    - Trigger: Click "Add to Cart"
    - Shows: Cart items, total, checkout button
    - Closes: Outside click or checkout
