## 3. DMCA POLICY (Copyright Compliance)

### Digital Millennium Copyright Act Compliance:

#### 3.1 Copyright Infringement Reporting

**If you believe content infringes your copyright:**

1. Send DMCA notice to: dmca@producersavenue.com
2. Include:
   - Your contact information
   - Description of copyrighted work
   - URL of infringing content on platform
   - Statement of good faith belief
   - Statement that information is accurate
   - Your physical or electronic signature

#### 3.2 Our Response Process

**Upon receiving valid DMCA notice:**
1. Remove or disable infringing content within 24 hours
2. Notify uploader of takedown
3. Terminate repeat infringers (3 strikes)

#### 3.3 Counter-Notification

**If you believe content was wrongly removed:**
1. Send counter-notice to dmca@producersavenue.com
2. Include:
   - Your contact information
   - Description of removed content
   - Statement under penalty of perjury that removal was mistake
   - Consent to jurisdiction
   - Your signature

3. We forward to copyright claimant
4. Content restored in 10-14 days unless claimant files lawsuit

#### 3.4 Designated DMCA Agent
- Name: [Your name or designated agent]
- Email: dmca@producersavenue.com
- Address: [Your business address]

#### 3.5 Repeat Infringer Policy
- First violation: Warning + content removed
- Second violation: 7-day suspension
- Third violation: Permanent ban

### 6.1 How Trust Score Works

**Starting Score:** 50 (neutral)

- Email verified: +10
- Phone verified: +10 (Phase 2)
- ID verified: +15
- 10+ sales: +10
- Rating 4.5+: +10
- Account 90+ days old: +5
- Active community member: +5

**Negative Factors:**
- Dispute opened against seller: -20
- 3+ user reports: -15
- Refund rate >20%: -25
- Failed payout verification: -30
- DMCA strike: -40

**Score Range:** 0-100

### 6.2 Actions by Trust Score

**90-100 (Excellent):**
- All features unlocked
- Instant payouts (Phase 2)
- Featured placement eligible
- Higher search ranking

**70-89 (Good):**
- Normal access
- Standard payout processing

**50-69 (Fair):**
- Delayed payouts (7-day hold)
- Upload limits enforced strictly
- More frequent manual reviews

**30-49 (Poor):**
- All products require manual approval before going live
- Payouts require admin approval
- Cannot create new products until score improves

**0-29 (Banned):**
- Account restricted
- Contact support required to regain access
- All products hidden

### 6.3 Improving Trust Score

**Users can:**
- Complete verification (ID, phone)
- Make successful sales
- Get positive reviews
- Be active and helpful in community
- Avoid disputes and reports

**Score recalculated:**
- After every transaction
- After every review received
- Daily (for time-based factors)

## 7. DISPUTE RESOLUTION PROCESS

### 7.1 When Disputes Arise

**Valid Dispute Reasons:**
- Seller didn't deliver product/service
- Product/service doesn't match description
- Product file is corrupted/broken
- Seller not responding to messages

**Invalid Dispute Reasons:**
- Buyer changed their mind (no refunds on delivered digital products)
- Buyer doesn't like the product (subjective)
- Buyer asking for additional free work

### 7.2 Dispute Process

**Step 1 - Direct Resolution (48 hours):**
- Buyer and seller try to resolve via messages
- Seller can: Re-upload file, provide additional delivery, offer partial refund

**Step 2 - Platform Mediation (72 hours):**
- If unresolved, buyer opens formal dispute
- Both parties submit evidence
- Admin reviews and makes decision

**Step 3 - Final Decision:**
- Admin decides: Full refund, partial refund, or no refund
- Decision is final
- Platform keeps commission either way (to prevent abuse)

### 7.3 Evidence Required

**Buyer must provide:**
- Order number
- Description of issue
- Screenshots/proof of problem
- Communication attempts with seller

**Seller must provide:**
- Proof of delivery
- Screenshots of communications
- Explanation of issue

### 7.4 Outcomes

**Refund Issued:**
- Money returned to buyer
- Seller's trust score: -20
- If 3+ disputes, account review

**No Refund:**
- Buyer receives nothing
- Marked as completed
- If buyer abuses disputes (3+), account review
